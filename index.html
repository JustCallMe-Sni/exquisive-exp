The Tournament

Born for
The Elite

Exquisive is not merely a competition — it is a statement. Elite sports and e-sports disciplines, world-class infrastructure, and an atmosphere that demands nothing short of perfection. From the opening ceremony to the grand finals, every moment is curated for greatness.

⚡

Sports

Two elite physical disciplines with world-class officiating and facilities. Badminton and Basketball — each a battlefield of skill, stamina, and strategy.

  • Badminton — May 23 & 24
  • Basketball — May 9 & 10
🎮

E-Sports

Three high-octane e-sports tournaments running simultaneously across six days. Compete, spectate, and witness digital excellence at its peak.

  • Valorant Skirmish
  • Minecraft Bedwars
  • COD Mobile 2v2
🌟

Experience

Beyond competition — a curated luxury experience. VIP hospitality, live broadcast streaming, player lounges, and post-event gala. Hyderabad's finest venue, elevated.
